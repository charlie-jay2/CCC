Information Area

Churchill Community College is a mixed comprehensive school in ROBLOX for 13 - 18 year olds, located in Wallsend, Newcastle, England. We aim to achieve to bring communities together through educating pupils. We work to bring our students a brighter future, and prepare them for the wonders and journeys they will take later in life.

We want all our students to value their personal development as well as pursue academic excellence. We offer a full and comprehensive extra-curricular programme which includes a variety of events and trips to assist our students in pursuing their dreams.

We nurture close ties with the local community to offer our students opportunities to develop a character marked by a deep moral integrity and refined social acumen. Our pupils emerge as individuals capable of making significant and lasting impacts not only within their local environment but also on national and global stages.
