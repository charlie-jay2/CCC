Exams

Churchill Community College aims for all students to get the best results possible. In our tenure of being a school, we have managed to get all students a pass in their exams wether that is English, Maths, Science. You name it. We will always offer support to students who are struggling in their exams, and we will always consult with carers, and the students on what they need to improve on and how they can.
